We area quality home inspection company based in Azle, Texas.  Our Home Inspectors are committed to you, the home buyer.  Our inspections are $265 for up to 2650 square feet and include a full home inspection plus one FREE optional inspection of your choice!  For homes over 2650 square feet, our price is 10¢/square foot.  

We service all of Azle, Tarrant County, Parker County and all of the surrounding cities and counties in DFW. The cities we service, include:  Azle, Springtown, Lake Worth, Lakeside, Keller, HEB, White Settlement, Aledo, Weatherford and everywhere else within 90 minutes of the DFW Metroplex.  See our coverage area here.

Our inspectors are professional Home Inspectors, licensed by the state of Texas to perform professional home inspections. We are also licensed by the Texas Department of Agriculture to perform Termite (WDI) Inspections (TDA# 0709518).  Please take a look below to view our prices and the optional systems that we inspect.  

Again, the first optional system is inspected for FREE & each additional optional system costs $10 each.  Also, please feel free to contact us via phone at (817) 400-6547 Text us at 682-302-2441, if you have any questions or if you would like to schedule a Home Inspection.  If you are convinced you would like to use our services, please click here, to schedule an inspection today.
