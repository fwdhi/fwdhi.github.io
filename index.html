WELCOME


We are a quality home inspection company based in Azle, Texas.  We believe in charging fair, affordable prices for home inspections.  Our inspections cost $265 for up to 2650 square feet and 10¢/square foot for homes over 2650 square feet.  All inspections include one FREE optional inspection of your choice (additional optional inspections cost $10 each more).  See our pricing page or our pricing tables below for additional details. 

Our home inspectors are committed to you, the home buyer.  We serve all of Tarrant County, Parker County, Dallas County, Denton County, Collin County and surrounding cities and counties in DFW Metroplex.  The cities we serve, include:  Azle, Springtown, Lake Worth, Lakeside, Keller, HEB, White Settlement, Aledo, Weatherford and everywhere else within 90 minutes
of the DFW Metroplex.  See our coverage area here.

All of our inspectors are professional Home Inspectors, licensed by the state of Texas to perform professional home inspections. We are also licensed by the Texas Department of Agriculture to perform Termite (WDI) Inspections (TDA# 0709518).  

Please take a few minutes to explore our website.  If you have questions, please feel free to contact us via phone at (817) 400-6547, or via text at (682) 302-2441 or via email at mc@fwdhi.com.   Also, you can schedule an inspection online, any time, 24/7 by clicking here.
