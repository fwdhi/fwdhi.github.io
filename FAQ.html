FAQ

These are some of the most common questions that we receive:

Why do your inspections cost less?

We pride ourselves on providing low cost, but not cheap, home inspections.  Our goal is NOT to make as much as we can from our home inspections, instead our goal is to provide an excellent value and to continue to build our business by providing quality home inspections at a great price.  When we provide a quality home inspection at a great price, word gets around.  The word of mouth that this creates, saves us a significant amount of money on marketing and we pass those savings on to you, our customer.

How long do you inpections take?

Since our inspections are very detailed, they generally take approximately about 4-5  hours to complete, excluding the time it takes to prepare the report.  The actual time may vary due to the size, age and condition of the house, as well as the number of optional systems (ie: pool, spa, well, septic tank, etc.) a house has.  When you schedule your inspection, we will do our best, based on our experience, to accurately estimate the time that your home inspection will take.  In a nutshell: we don't rush our inspections.

 How much does it cost to inspect the optional systems, like a pool, a sprinkler system, and built-in barbecue?

We will inspect the first optional system for FREE. For any of the home's other optional systems you would like inspected, we charge an additional $10 each.  Optional inspectiion options are:  Termite,  Crawlspace, Pool/Spa, Well, Septic systems, Outbuildings, or Built-in barbecues. Most other inspectors charge 3 to 7 times this rate for each of these inspections.

 Do you inspect condos and townhomes?

Yes we do.  Our pricing for these inspections is the same as for single family residences and is 10¢ per square feet  (Minimum charge, $265)

Should I attend the inspection?

Yes.  We recommend that you be present at the at least the last 1/2 hour of the inspection.  You are, however, welcome to accompany us during the entire inspection if that's what you prefer.  We do realize that it is not always possible for you to be present due to other obligations.  Don't worry:  your inspection report will be very detailed and your inspector will be available to answer any questions you might have, either in person, on the phone or via email.

 What if I am not completely satisfied with my inspection?

In the unlikely event that you are not completely satisfied with the inspection that we perform for you, we will do our best correct or resolve any complaints that you might have.  If you are still not completely satisfied with the inspection we perform, we will we refund your entire inspection fee.  See our Satisfaction Guarantee page for more details.
